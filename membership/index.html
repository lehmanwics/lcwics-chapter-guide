  • Club Membership

General membership applies to anyone who subscribes to our mailing list and club on Campus Link.

You can only be considered an active member if you are a general member who meets the WiCS points requirements (see points system below).

Sign-Ups

When a new member submits the welcome form (http://bit.ly/lcwicswelcome), they will automatically receive a welcome email from Mailchimp with a link to our Slack. The google form adds their email address to our Mailchimp mailing list via Zapier.

Membership Benefits

  • All members have the right to be informed about all planned WiCS events
  • All members have the right to benefit from our events such as networking opportunities with company representatives
  • All members have the right to make constructive suggestions or discuss any issues that may arise with the club or any board or committee member

We also provide support for one another and consider various activities throughout the year such as:

  • Professional workshops (resume review and behavioral interview prep, networking, etc.)
  • Technical workshops (Machine learning, data visualization, web development, etc.)
  • Technical Interview prep (learn to ace the technical interview and get offers rolling in!)
  • Corporate events (tours, info sessions, meet and greets)
  • Job and Internship Resources (check out our Slack and newsletter for most recent opportunities!)
  • Social Outings (dinner and movie nights, ice cream socials, informal gatherings, study breaks, etc.)

The chapter aims to provide the following additional benefits in the future:

  • GHC Conference Sponsorship
  • Educational Events (research opportunities, graduate school, other informational sessions)
  • Hackathons
  • Study groups, Tutoring, and Mentorship
  • Community outreach programs
  • Volunteering programs

Points for Active Membership

To be considered an "active member" of WiCS, you must attend, at least:

  • 5 general meetings or at least half of general meetings held during the semester, whichever is less.
  • 1 event hosted by WiCS or in partnership with WiCS

Each meeting counts as 1 point and each event also counts as 1 point. There is a friend bonus when you bring a friend to a meeting or event, you earn an additional point. The friend you bring only counts one time. You must bring a new friend to each meeting to earn more than one point.

Points do not roll over to the following semester(s).

All active members are eligible for end of semester awards and prizes.

National Membership

WiCS is a chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery. All members are welcomed and highly encouraged to sign up for a yearly membership to ACM and take advantage of all their benefits.

ACM Charter

The Lehman College Women in Computing & Computer Science club is a chartered club under the Association of Computing Machinery (ACM). The club is considered a non-profit charter under ACM.

The chapter's admin interface to edit members and board members can be accessed via: http://campus.acm.org/public/chapters/

The credentials for this interface are available in LastPass.

Annual finance reports and public LC WiCS events can also be submitted via this interface.

All finance records must be kept for end of year reporting.

Information on how to manage an ACM-W chapter is included here: http://www.acm.org/binaries/content/assets/chapters/chapter-in-a-box_acm-w.pdf

Lehman College Club

Lehman WiCS also exists as a registered club under Campus Link: https://lehman.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/lcwics

The club must be re-registered every semester via Campus Link to be considered an active club at Lehman College. The notification to re-register will be sent to the club's Gmail account.

## <span style="color: orange">*ACM Charter*</span>

The Lehman College Women in Computing & Computer Science club is a chartered club under the [Association of Computing Machinery (ACM)](https://women.acm.org/category/chapters/). The club is considered a non-profit charter under ACM.

The chapter's **admin interface** to edit members and board members can be accessed via: [http://campus.acm.org/public/chapters/](http://campus.acm.org/public/chapters/)

The credentials for this interface are available in **LastPass**.

Annual finance reports and public LC WiCS events can also be submitted via this interface. 

<span style="color: red">**All finance records must be kept for end of year reporting.**</span>

Information on how to manage an ACM-W chapter is included here: [http://www.acm.org/binaries/content/assets/chapters/chapter-in-a-box_acm-w.pdf](http://www.acm.org/binaries/content/assets/chapters/chapter-in-a-box_acm-w.pdf)

## <span style="color: orange">*Lehman College Club*</span>

Lehman WiCS also exists as a registered club under Campus Link: [https://lehman.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/lcwics](https://lehman.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/lcwics)

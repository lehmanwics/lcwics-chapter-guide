  • Newsletter

The chapter sends out a weekly newsletter via Mailchimp to all members using the Lehman WiCS list.

The newsletter consists of upcoming LC WiCS and external events, highlighted opportunities, and any important news.

Events are curated from the following sources:

  • "#events" channel on Slack
  • Gary's Guide
  • Meetup.com
  • Eventbrite.com
  • Outside collaborators asking to share events with our members

Responsibility: Communications Chair is responsible for weekly newsletter.

Structure of Newsletter

A "Newsletter" template is available in Mailchimp under "Templates" to guide you on the structure and design of the newsletter. Use this template for newsletters.

The suggested structure is as follows:

  • Header image
  • Welcome message with a couple of sentences describing content of newsletter
  • Social media link icons
  • Bulleted list of newsletter content to follow
    • Example:
      • [LCWICS] General Body Meeting + Weekly Minutes
      • [LCWiCS] Build a Raspberry Pi Cloud Server
      • [LCWiCS x NSBE/SHPE] Demystify the Technical Interview: How to win at interviewing
      • [Goldman Sachs] Application for Software Engineering Internship
  • Content blocks
  • Footer content

Past Newsletters

Past newsletters should be saved under the "Newsletters" folder via Mailchimp. New members cannot receive past newsletters but they can view them as a link.
